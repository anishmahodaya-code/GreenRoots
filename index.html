Featured Plantation Drives

Pune Green Drive

Our Pune Green Drive focused on restoring green cover in urban areas through community participation and awareness programs. Over 5,000 saplings were planted with the help of local volunteers.

Mumbai Coastal Plantation

This plantation initiative aimed to improve coastal biodiversity and create greener public spaces. Volunteers planted native species suited to the coastal environment.

Wardha Forest Restoration

A large-scale effort to restore degraded forest land and strengthen the ecological balance of the region. Hundreds of volunteers participated throughout the drive.

Why Trees Matter

Trees are one of nature's most powerful tools for combating climate change. They absorb carbon dioxide, improve air quality, support biodiversity and help communities build a healthier future.
